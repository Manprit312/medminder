We’re planning a way for you to invite doctors, nurses, or pharmacists to view read-only adherence summaries — so they can see whether reminders are helping, not to replace your medical record.

Two separate experiences

Patients and clinicians would use different apps / sign-ins — your data wouldn’t be mixed with a hospital login you already have unless we build that integration later.
